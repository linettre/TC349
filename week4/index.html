About Trevor

My name is Trevor Linet, and I attend Michigan State University. My major is Computer Science, with a cognate in telecommunications. I am currently a senior, but will have to attend a fifth year! I enjoy playing Xbox, PC, and Playstation with my friends, as well as Kayaking, fishing, and Disc Golf. I also Enjoy watching cartoons and hanging out with a group of friends just to relax. When I'm working, I like to program and fix computers, because that is what I am good at; and it is something I enjoy.
